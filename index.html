What is T.Hacks?

T.Hacks is a 12 hour hackathon for high school and university students, taking place in Toronto, Canada. Throughout the event, there will be workshops on different technologies, ranging from introductions to languages like Python and Java, to more advanced topics, such as Android and Node.js.

Why Attend?

At T.Hacks, you'll receive the oppertunity to meet other software developers from abroad and to make new friends. In addition, we guarantee that you'll learn something new at our workshops or amongsts new peers. Industry professionals will also be speaking at this event and will be more than happy to answer any questions you may have in their profession.
